Crop Recommendations for Humid Weather, Clayey Soil, and High Irrigation

Rice

Description: Rice thrives in humid weather and clayey soil. High irrigation ensures proper paddy field conditions.

Growth Period: 100-150 days

Yield: High

Special Notes: Requires waterlogged conditions and nitrogen-rich fertilizers.

Sugarcane

Description: Sugarcane grows well in humid conditions and clayey soil. High irrigation supports its high water requirements.

Growth Period: 10-12 months

Yield: Very High

Special Notes: Requires deep soil and regular weeding.

Banana

Description: Bananas prefer humid weather and clayey soil. High irrigation ensures healthy fruit development.

Growth Period: 9-12 months

Yield: High

Special Notes: Requires well-drained soil and protection from strong winds.

Taro

Description: Taro grows well in humid weather and clayey soil. High irrigation is essential for its water-loving nature.

Growth Period: 6-12 months

Yield: Moderate to High

Special Notes: Requires consistent moisture and partial shade.

Maize

Description: Maize can grow in humid weather and clayey soil. High irrigation ensures proper growth during dry spells.

Growth Period: 90-120 days

Yield: High

Special Notes: Requires proper drainage to avoid waterlogging.
