Crop Recommendations for Humid Weather, Loamy Soil, and Low Irrigation

Millet

Description: Millet thrives in humid weather and loamy soil. It is drought-resistant and requires low irrigation.

Growth Period: 60-90 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Ideal for areas with limited water availability.

Sorghum

Description: Sorghum grows well in humid weather and loamy soil. It is highly drought-tolerant and requires minimal irrigation.

Growth Period: 90-120 days

Yield: Moderate to High

Special Notes: Used for grain, fodder, and biofuel.

Chickpea

Description: Chickpeas grow well in humid weather and loamy soil. They are drought-resistant and require low irrigation.

Growth Period: 90-110 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Fixes nitrogen in the soil, improving soil fertility.

Sunflower

Description: Sunflowers thrive in humid weather and loamy soil. They are drought-resistant and require low irrigation.

Growth Period: 70-100 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Barley

Description: Barley grows well in humid weather and loamy soil. It is drought-tolerant and requires low irrigation.

Growth Period: 90-120 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Ideal for areas with limited water availability.
