Crop Recommendations for Humid Weather, Clayey Soil, and Low Irrigation

Rice

Description: Rice thrives in humid weather and clayey soil. It can tolerate waterlogged conditions, but low irrigation is sufficient for certain varieties.

Growth Period: 100-150 days

Yield: High

Special Notes: Choose drought-resistant rice varieties for low irrigation.

Barley

Description: Barley grows well in humid weather and clayey soil. It is drought-tolerant and requires low irrigation.

Growth Period: 90-120 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Ideal for areas with limited water availability.

Oats

Description: Oats are well-suited for humid weather and clayey soil. They require minimal irrigation and can grow in cooler climates.

Growth Period: 90-110 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Used for both human consumption and animal feed.

Mustard

Description: Mustard grows well in humid weather and clayey soil. It is drought-resistant and requires low irrigation.

Growth Period: 80-100 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Used for oil extraction and as a spice.

Chickpea

Description: Chickpeas grow well in humid weather and clayey soil. They are drought-resistant and require low irrigation.

Growth Period: 90-110 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Fixes nitrogen in the soil, improving soil fertility.
