Crop Recommendations for Sunny Weather, Clayey Soil, and Low Irrigation

Wheat

Description: Wheat grows well in sunny weather and clayey soil. It can tolerate low irrigation due to its deep root system.

Growth Period: 110-130 days

Yield: Moderate to High

Special Notes: Requires well-drained soil and minimal waterlogging.

Barley

Description: Barley is well-suited for sunny weather and clayey soil. It is drought-tolerant and requires low irrigation.

Growth Period: 90-120 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Ideal for areas with limited water availability.

Sunflower

Description: Sunflowers thrive in sunny weather and clayey soil. They are drought-resistant and require low irrigation.

Growth Period: 70-100 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Sorghum

Description: Sorghum is highly adaptable to sunny weather and clayey soil. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal irrigation.

Growth Period: 90-120 days

Yield: Moderate to High

Special Notes: Excellent for arid and semi-arid regions.

Chickpea

Description: Chickpeas grow well in sunny weather and clayey soil. They are drought-resistant and require low irrigation.

Growth Period: 90-110 days

Yield: Moderate

Special Notes: Fixes nitrogen in the soil, improving soil fertility.
