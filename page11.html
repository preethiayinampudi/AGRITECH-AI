Crop Recommendations for Sunny Weather, Clayey Soil, and High Irrigation

Corn

Description: Corn thrives in sunny weather and clayey soil. High irrigation ensures optimal growth and yield.

Growth Period: 90-120 days

Yield: High

Special Notes: Requires nitrogen-rich fertilizers and regular watering.

Soybeans

Description: Soybeans grow well in sunny weather and clayey soil. High irrigation supports healthy pod formation.

Growth Period: 80-120 days

Yield: High

Special Notes: Fixes nitrogen in the soil, improving fertility for future crops.

Cotton

Description: Cotton prefers sunny weather and clayey soil. High irrigation is essential for fiber development.

Growth Period: 150-180 days

Yield: High

Special Notes: Requires pest management due to susceptibility to bollworms.

Sunflower

Description: Sunflowers thrive in sunny weather and clayey soil. High irrigation ensures healthy flower and seed development.

Growth Period: 70-100 days

Yield: Moderate to High

Special Notes: Requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Sorghum

Description: Sorghum is highly adaptable to sunny weather and clayey soil. High irrigation ensures proper growth during dry spells.

Growth Period: 90-120 days

Yield: Moderate to High

Special Notes: Excellent for arid and semi-arid regions.
